Important Notes:

  • FHA self-sufficiency rules apply to 3-4 unit properties ONLY. Duplexes (2 units) do not qualify for rental income offset under FHA guidelines.
  • For qualifying 3-4 unit properties, FHA allows 75% of rental income as an offset when you occupy one unit
  • Rental income must be documented with lease agreements and market rent analysis
  • You must have documented landlord experience or reserves typically equal to 6 months PITI
  • Lender may require additional documentation and reserves for self-sufficiency claims
  • This calculator is for estimation purposes only - consult with an FHA-approved lender for final approval
